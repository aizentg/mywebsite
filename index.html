About

A mind that runs
on curiosity.

I'm a student of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning — but that's only one layer. I paint. I photograph. I code. I collect records. I think about the aesthetics of everything, from loss functions to brushstrokes.

Born and rooted in Kathmandu, Nepal, shaped by the textures of ancient temples and modern code. My work tries to honor that richness — the ornate, the coded, the deeply human.
